HOW IT WORKS

From ticker to verdict in seconds

WHY VELU

Not data. Not opinions. A verdict.

Other tools give you charts and leave the thinking to you. Velu does the analysis and tells you what it concludes — then shows its work.

⚡

The Edge Screener

Automatically surfaces stocks the market has oversold relative to their fundamentals. Your daily list of asymmetric opportunities.

◈

Three-agent analysis

Every stock is analyzed from three angles — macro positioning, quantitative valuation, and downside risk — before a verdict is reached.

◎

Clear Buy/Hold/Sell

No hedging, no "it depends." A directional call with conviction level, price target, and the reasoning behind it.

⊹

Scenario modeling

Bull, base, and bear cases with probabilities and price targets for each — so you understand the full distribution, not just the point estimate.

◇

Live market data

Real-time prices, P/E, margins, ROE, and 52-week ranges pulled fresh on every analysis. Never stale.

▨

Devil's advocate

The Tail Risk agent actively argues against the thesis, surfacing what could go wrong before you commit capital.
